Welcome to the Schrondinger's Catverse!

Here is how this game works.

Cats are roaming in the wild without food and play. Your task is to bring the cats into the cage by dragging them into it. You have to hurry, because as the clock ticks, the cats lose XP. Catch those cats moving towards the walls as colliding into them causes more XP loss.

Rules of the game:

      All cats do not have the same starting XP.
      Cats will not leave the cage once inside and their XP stops dropping. They are safe!
      When a cat XP drops to zero, it dies. When three or more cats die, it's GAME OVER.
